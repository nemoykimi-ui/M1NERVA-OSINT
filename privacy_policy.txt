# Privacy Policy for M1NERVA

**Version 1.0**  
**Effective Date:** 2026-07-10

---

## 1. Introduction

M1NERVA ("the Software") is an OSINT aggregation tool that operates locally on your device. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use the Software.

## 2. Data Collection by the Software

**The Software does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.**

All operations performed by the Software occur locally on your device. The Software does not:

- Send any data to servers controlled by the developers.
- Collect telemetry, usage statistics, or crash reports.
- Store or log your searches except as explicitly configured by you.

## 3. Data You Input

When you use the Software, you may input:

- Phone numbers
- Email addresses
- Usernames
- IP addresses
- Full names
- Addresses
- Photos (locally processed)

This data is used only to perform the specific searches you request. It is temporarily processed in memory and may be saved to local report files (in the `reports/` directory) if you choose to save results.

## 4. Third-Party APIs

The Software makes queries to third-party APIs using the API keys you provide. When you use the Software:

- Your queries are sent directly to these third-party services.
- The developers of M1NERVA have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the privacy practices of these third-party services.
- You should review the privacy policies of each third-party service you use.

## 5. Local Data Storage

The Software may generate and store:

- **Logs:** Debug and error logs (in `logs/` directory).
- **Reports:** Search results (in `reports/` directory).

All such data is stored locally on your device. **You are responsible for**:

- Securing this data.
- Managing its retention and deletion.
- Complying with applicable privacy laws regarding any personal data contained in these files.

## 6. API Keys

Your API keys are stored locally in `api_keys.json`. They are not transmitted to the developers of M1NERVA. You are responsible for securing your API keys and for complying with the terms of service of each provider.

## 7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, security is your responsibility. We recommend:

- Using full-disk encryption on your device.
- Not sharing your `api_keys.json` or `reports/` directory with unauthorized parties.
- Regularly deleting old reports and logs if they contain sensitive information.

## 8. Data Retention

We do not have access to your data. You decide how long to keep reports and logs on your own device.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Software is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Software after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
